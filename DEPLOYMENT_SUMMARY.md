## 🎯 Next Steps

### Deploy to Vercel (5 minutes)

1. **Go to Vercel**
   - Visit [vercel.com](https://vercel.com)
   - Sign in with GitHub

2. **Import Project**
   - Click "New Project"
   - Select `satvik-sharma-05/portfolio`
   - Vercel will auto-detect Next.js

3. **Deploy**
   - Click "Deploy"
   - Wait 2-3 minutes
   - Your site will be live!

4. **Get Your URL**
   - You'll get a URL like: `portfolio-satvik.vercel.app`
   - Share it with the world! 🎉

### Optional: Custom Domain

1. Buy a domain (e.g., satvikharma.com)
2. In Vercel: Settings → Domains
3. Add your domain
4. Update DNS records
5. Wait for propagation
